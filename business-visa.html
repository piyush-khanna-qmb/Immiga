Business Visa Services

Expert guidance to secure business visas for global opportunities, including Gulf countries, Europe, Russia, and Australia.

A business visa allows entrepreneurs, investors, and business owners to establish and manage operations in foreign countries. It opens doors to global markets, partnerships, and growth opportunities.

At ImmigaTech Services, we provide end-to-end assistance to secure business visas in various countries. Whether you're expanding your business, starting a new venture, or exploring investment opportunities, our experts will ensure that your application meets all requirements.

We guide you through the application process, ensuring that your business visa application is complete, accurate, and aligned with the specific country requirements.

Available Countries

United Arab Emirates (UAE)

The UAE offers business visas for entrepreneurs and investors aiming to establish businesses in Dubai, Abu Dhabi, and other cities with lucrative tax benefits and a thriving economy.

Germany

Germany offers business visas for entrepreneurs, investors, and freelancers seeking to start or invest in businesses in the country. A strong economy and strategic location in Europe make it an ideal business hub.

Russia

Russia provides business visas to foreign entrepreneurs and investors looking to explore business opportunities in one of the world's largest economies.

Australia

Australia offers several pathways for business owners and investors, including the Business Innovation and Investment Visa, to promote entrepreneurship and growth within the country's economy.

Long-Term Benefits of Obtaining a Business Visa

Global Business Expansion

A business visa provides access to new markets and opportunities, allowing you to expand your business internationally and grow your global network.

Investment and Funding Opportunities

Many countries offer various investment incentives, grants, and funding opportunities for foreign businesses, helping you secure financial resources for growth.

Access to Skilled Talent

With a business visa, you can hire skilled workers from around the world, allowing your company to benefit from diverse talent and expertise.

Long-Term Residency Pathway

In many cases, a business visa can serve as a stepping stone to permanent residency, offering long-term stability and residency options for business owners and their families.

Business Visa Application Process

  • Business Idea Development

    We assist in refining your business idea to ensure it meets the requirements for a business visa. Whether you're setting up a new company or investing in an existing one, we help you create a solid business plan.

  • Visa Requirements Assessment

    Our experts will evaluate your business plan and determine the specific visa requirements for your chosen country, including financial thresholds, business experience, and documentation.

  • Financial Planning

    We help you gather the necessary financial documentation, including proof of funds, investment plans, and financial projections, ensuring you meet the country's economic eligibility criteria.

  • Business Documentation

    Our team will help you prepare all necessary documents, including business registration papers, partnership agreements, tax returns, and proof of previous business activities if applicable.

  • Visa Application Form Completion

    We assist with filling out your business visa application forms, ensuring accuracy and completeness to avoid delays or rejections.

  • Visa Submission and Payment

    Our consultants will guide you through the visa submission process, review your documentation, and ensure that all visa fees are paid in full, avoiding any hiccups in the process.

  • Interview and Pitch Preparation

    For countries requiring an interview, we help you prepare by practicing your pitch, business plan, and financials to ensure you're fully prepared to impress the immigration authorities.

  • Post-Visa Approval Support

    Once your business visa is approved, we provide pre-departure services, including guidance on setting up your business in the destination country, legal compliance, tax regulations, and more.

Required Documents

Personal Documents

  • Valid passport
  • Birth certificate
  • Police clearance certificate
  • Medical examination reports (if applicable)

Financial Documents

  • Proof of funds (bank statements)
  • Investment proof (business plans, funding, etc.)
  • Tax records and financial statements

Business Documents

  • Business registration certificate
  • Business plans and investment proposals
  • Proof of business ownership or partnership
  • Revenue/profit statements (if applicable)

Note: Requirements may vary by country. Our consultants will provide a customized document checklist based on your business visa application destination.
