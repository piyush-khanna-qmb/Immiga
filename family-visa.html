Family Visa Services

Your essential guide to family reunification, visa requirements, and application procedures for bringing your loved ones to foreign countries.

A family visa is an official document that allows the members of your family to join you in a foreign country. This type of visa is typically granted to spouses, children, parents, and sometimes other close relatives. Each country has specific requirements for family reunification, which vary based on local immigration laws and the applicant's status in the country.

At ImmigaTech Services, we offer expert guidance through the family visa application process. Whether you're seeking to reunite with family in the Gulf, Europe, Russia, or Australia, our experienced consultants are equipped with the latest information on visa policies and requirements for each region.

Our team helps you prepare the necessary documentation, understand eligibility criteria, and navigate the entire application process to make family reunification a smooth and stress-free experience.

Available Countries

United Arab Emirates

The UAE offers a family visa program that allows expatriates to bring their spouses, children, and sometimes even parents to join them in the country. It's a popular option for foreign workers in the Gulf, providing a pathway for family reunification.

Saudi Arabia

Saudi Arabia's family visa allows foreign workers to bring their families to live with them in the Kingdom. This visa can include spouses, children, and sometimes even other dependents. The application process involves proving financial stability and legal residency.

Australia

Australia's family visa options allow citizens and permanent residents to sponsor their partners, children, and other family members. The process requires demonstrating sufficient income, relationship status, and the ability to support the sponsored family members.

Germany

Germany offers family reunification visas for spouses, children, and other close relatives of German residents and citizens. The requirements focus on income levels, housing conditions, and language skills.

Russia

Russia allows foreign nationals to bring their families to join them through a family visa. This is possible for spouses, children, and parents of individuals who hold long-term residence permits in Russia.

Long-Term Benefits of a Family Visa

Family Reunification

A family visa provides the opportunity to reunite with your loved ones, ensuring that you and your family can live together in your new country, fostering emotional support and stability.

Better Quality of Life

Bringing your family to live with you can improve the quality of life for everyone involved, offering better living conditions, education, and healthcare opportunities in the destination country.

Access to Social Benefits

In many countries, family visa holders gain access to social services such as healthcare, education, and other government benefits, providing peace of mind and security for the entire family.

Pathway to Permanent Residency

Family visa holders often have the opportunity to apply for permanent residency or citizenship after living in the country for a certain period, allowing you and your family to fully integrate and settle long-term.

Required Documents

Personal Documents

  • Valid passport with at least 6 months validity
  • Completed visa application form
  • Passport-sized photographs meeting specific requirements
  • Marriage certificate (for spouse applications)
  • Birth certificates of children (for dependent children)
  • Proof of relationship (photos, communication history, etc.)

Financial Documents

  • Proof of sufficient income or employment to support family
  • Bank statements
  • Proof of accommodation (rental agreement, housing offer)
  • Sponsorship letter (if applicable)

Additional Documents

  • Health/travel insurance for family members
  • Police clearance certificates (if required)
  • Educational or employment records (for dependents above a certain age)

Note: Document requirements vary by country. Our consultants will provide a tailored checklist based on your specific circumstances and destination country.
