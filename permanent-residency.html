Permanent Residency Services

Comprehensive assistance for obtaining permanent residency in countries like Canada, Australia, and Europe.

Permanent residency allows foreign nationals to live and work indefinitely in a country without becoming a citizen. It provides access to many social benefits, including healthcare, education, and legal rights.

At ImmigaTech Services, we specialize in helping individuals and families obtain permanent residency through various immigration programs. Our team ensures that your application meets the eligibility criteria and is submitted with all necessary documents.

Whether you are applying for skilled migration, family sponsorship, or investor programs, we provide end-to-end guidance to make your journey smooth and successful.

Available Countries

Canada

Canada offers various pathways to permanent residency, including Express Entry, Provincial Nominee Programs (PNPs), and family sponsorships.

Australia

Australia provides permanent residency through skilled migration visas, family sponsorship, and business investor programs.

Germany

Germany offers permanent residency to skilled workers, entrepreneurs, and family members under specific residency permits.

Long-Term Benefits of Permanent Residency

Access to Social Services

Permanent residents enjoy access to essential public services such as healthcare, education, and welfare, ensuring a high quality of life in their new country.

Job Security and Employment Rights

As a permanent resident, you can work freely in your host country without restrictions, opening up a wide range of job opportunities across various industries.

Path to Citizenship

Permanent residency is often a stepping stone to citizenship, offering individuals and families a pathway to full legal rights, including voting and holding office.

Family Sponsorship

Permanent residents can sponsor close family members (such as spouses and children) for permanent residency, enabling family reunification and a stable future together.

Permanent Residency Application Process

  • Eligibility Assessment

    Our consultants will evaluate your eligibility for permanent residency based on factors like age, education, work experience, language proficiency, and more. We’ll guide you through the specific requirements of your target country.

  • Document Collection

    We assist in gathering all required documents, including your passport, educational certificates, work experience letters, and financial records, ensuring everything is in order for your application.

  • Application Form Completion

    Our team helps you complete the detailed application forms accurately, ensuring that all information is correctly entered to avoid delays or complications.

  • Language Proficiency Tests

    Depending on the country, you may need to provide proof of language proficiency (e.g., IELTS, TOEFL). We help you prepare for these tests and ensure you meet the required scores.

  • Medical and Police Checks

    Permanent residency applications often require medical exams and police clearance certificates. We guide you through the process of obtaining these documents, ensuring all requirements are met.

  • Visa Submission

    We assist in submitting your application package to the relevant authorities, ensuring that all documents are properly organized and that the application is submitted within the deadlines.

  • Interview Preparation

    Some countries may require an interview as part of the permanent residency process. We help you prepare for the interview, reviewing potential questions and providing guidance on how to present your case effectively.

  • Post-Approval Guidance

    Once your permanent residency is approved, we offer guidance on settling in your new country, including advice on accommodation, health insurance, banking, and integration into local society.

Required Documents

Personal Documents

  • Valid passport
  • Birth certificate
  • Police clearance certificate
  • Medical examination reports
  • Marriage certificate (if applicable)

Financial Documents

  • Proof of funds (bank statements)
  • Income tax returns
  • Property valuation reports (if applicable)

Professional Documents

  • Educational certificates
  • Work experience letters
  • Language proficiency test results (IELTS, TOEFL, etc.)

Note: Requirements may vary by country. Our consultants will provide a customized document checklist based on your chosen immigration pathway.
